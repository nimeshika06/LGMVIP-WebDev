About US

NSL a trusted name in chemical production. Headquartered in Hydrerabad, NSL is the chemical manufacturer. The company supports its customers and suppliers with tailor-made distribution solutions for industrial and speciality chemicals. With over 100 products and a world-class supplier base.

NSL follows a continued profitable growth strategy targeting selected customer industries and focusing on taking over activities from chemical producers seeking outsourcing options. In addition the company constantly seeks new opportunities with a focus on the growth areas of Asia, Latin America and Eastern Europe, to capture the expected strong growth in demand for chemicals in these regions.

The company is led by an experienced board of management combining decades of expertise in the chemical distribution and manufacturing sector. Below are the core management team that is highly experienced in chemical distribution, characterized by clear commitments to profitable growth and safety, with strong track records and project management skills.
